About Hackathon

Hack4Good is an upcoming 7 days hackathon with the aim of building the solutions to humanity's greatest problems which would eventually promote social causes. Whether you're a seasoned hacker or this is your first time, we invite you to come up with an innovative solution to real-world issues facing not-for-profit organisation.

Hackathon Timeline

10th Jan, 2022 - 15th Jan, 2022

Registration & Selection Of The Problem Statement

In this period, participants can register themselves in a team and select any of the problem statements.

21st Jan, 2022

Round-1 Submission Deadline

In this round, participants will be required to make a working model so as to present to solution on the selected problem statement.

22nd Jan, 2022

Evaluation

Based on Presentation, Novelty, Uniqueness and Applicability of solution, the projects will be evaluated.

23rd Jan, 2022

Finalists Declaration

Announcement of the selected teams.

23rd Jan, 2022

Preparation Day

Final presentation edits and preparation day for the participants.

24th Jan, 2022

Hack4Good Finale

Presentation by the selected teams and Announcement of Winners.

FAQ

  • How much does it cost to take part in this program?

    Hack4Good is an absolutely free hackathon, you can directly participate by applying.

  • Can I propose an already existing solution?

    Nope, you can’t, the solution should be the original one generated by you.

  • Why should we participate in Hack4Good?

    Hack4Good is a 7 days hackathon that will be held on a virtual platform, which means it provides you an opportunity to participate directly from your home, with no fuss of going anywhere. So just build a solution to solve humanity's greatest problems and win prizes up to Rs. 45,000/-.

  • What is eligibility?

    All students are allowed to participate in this hackathon.

  • How do we contact Hack4Good organisers?

    You can simply drop an email to hack4good@ieee-cis-sbc.org with all of your questions and your queries will be answered shortly.
